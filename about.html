Background

Hi, I'm Faith. I'm an engineering graduate from JKUAT, currently pursuing a Master of Science in Business Analytics with the Quantic School of Business and Technology (Class of 2027). I'm a data professional who works at the intersection of analytics, governance, and impact. My work transforms complex data into clear insights that drive ethical AI adoption, sustainable decision-making, and responsible innovation.

I have hands-on experience across the full data lifecycle, from designing monitoring and evaluation frameworks to building dashboards and analytics solutions that are both rigorous and accessible. I've led projects bridging technical analysis with strategic outcomes, always ensuring data is collected, governed, and used with integrity and purpose.

I'm particularly passionate about how data can serve both people and the planet. Whether I'm implementing governance frameworks, analyzing datasets, or enabling AI adoption in responsible ways, I approach every project with a commitment to transparency, equity, and measurable impact.

My approach is rooted in precision and purpose. I treat every dataset as a story waiting to be told, every framework as an opportunity to build trust, and every project as a chance to create change that matters. I believe that meaningful progress comes from combining technical excellence with unwavering ethical standards.

What I Do

I provide end-to-end data leadership that turns information into impact.

From Strategy to Systems:

I design and implement robust data collection and governance frameworks that ensure quality, ethics, and efficiency from the very start, building a trusted foundation for all analysis.

From Chaos to Clarity:

I specialize in transforming complex, messy datasets into clear strategic insights. My analysis identifies patterns, trends, and opportunities that directly inform high-stakes decisions.

From Insight to Action:

I bridge the gap between data and decisions. Through compelling visualization, storytelling, and stakeholder engagement, I translate technical findings into actionable narratives that drive alignment and measurable business outcomes.

From Activity to Impact:

I build monitoring and evaluation frameworks that move beyond tracking activity to demonstrating real progress and ROI, ensuring strategies are adaptive and resources are focused on what works.

Throughout, I lead:

I manage projects and cross-functional teams to deliver these solutions on time and aligned with organizational goals, ensuring a seamless journey from data to value.
